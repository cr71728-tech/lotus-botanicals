Quality Assurance

At Lotus Botanicals LLP, quality is embedded into every stage of our operations. We implement internationally benchmarked Quality Management Systems (QMS) to consistently deliver products that meet global regulatory and customer expectations.

Key Quality Practices

  • Globally aligned Quality Management Systems (QMS)
  • Strong organizational culture focused on compliance
  • Strict adherence to Good Documentation Practices (GDP)
  • Uniform QA oversight across all functions
  • Quality by Design (QbD), e-quality systems, vendor qualification
  • Annual Product Quality Review (APQR)
  • Robust product release procedures ensuring safety & compliance
