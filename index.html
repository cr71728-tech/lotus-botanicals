Manufacturing Process Flow

  1. Raw Material Receiving: Incoming inspection and traceability logging.
  2. Pre-processing: Cleaning, drying, and size reduction.
  3. Extraction / Formulation: Controlled extraction or blending operations.
  4. Filtration & Concentration: Remove solids and concentrate active fractions.
  5. Drying & Standardization: Achieve required moisture and potency.
  6. Filling & Packaging: Fill, label, and package with tamper-evident seals.
  7. Final QC & Dispatch: Final testing, batch release, and shipment.

Each step follows documented SOPs with in-process checks and batch records.

Quality Policy & Assurance

Our commitment is to supply safe, effective, and compliant botanical products through robust quality systems.

  • Policy: We maintain documented quality objectives, continuous improvement, and compliance with applicable standards and regulations.
  • Assurance: Batch traceability, validated processes, and release testing (identity, purity, potency, contaminants).
  • Quality Systems: SOPs, internal audits, corrective actions (CAPA), and staff training programs.

Safety

Employee and product safety are prioritized through engineering controls, PPE, and training.

  • Mandatory PPE (gloves, goggles, aprons) in processing areas
  • Lockout/tagout procedures for maintenance
  • Emergency response plans and periodic drills
  • Hazard communication and chemical safety data sheet (SDS) availability

Report any safety incidents immediately to the floor supervisor. Confidential reporting options are available.
