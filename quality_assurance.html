Quality Assurance

At Lotus Botanicals LLP, quality is embedded into every stage of our operations. We implement internationally benchmarked Quality Management Systems (QMS) to consistently deliver products that meet global regulatory and customer expectations. Our teams maintain a strong culture of documentation, compliance, and continuous improvement.

Key Quality Practices

  • Implementation of globally aligned Quality Management Systems (QMS)
  • Strong organizational culture focused on quality and compliance
  • Strict adherence to Good Documentation Practices (GDP)
  • Uniform QA oversight across quality control, compliance, training, and turnaround times
  • Quality by Design (QbD), electronic quality systems, vendor qualification, and self-inspections
  • Annual Product Quality Review (APQR) and continual process improvement
  • Robust product release procedures ensuring safety, consistency, and regulatory compliance
